### Core Concepts: Decentralized Identifiers, Verifiable Credentials, and Self-Sovereign Identity
At the heart of Web3 Identity are three core concepts: Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs), Verifiable Credentials (VCs), and Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI). DIDs are unique identifiers that allow users to create and manage their own decentralized identities. VCs are digital certificates that verify the authenticity of a user's identity, while SSI refers to the ability of users to control their own identity data, free from centralized authorities. These concepts work together to create a decentralized identity management system that is secure, private, and efficient.
