## 6. Calculating the True Net APY

Many dashboards quote **gross APR** (e.g., 35 %). To see what lands in your wallet, factor in:

1. **Gas Fees per Cycle** – On Polygon, a typical **stake/unstake** cycle costs **$0.05**.
2. **Token Price Volatility** – Convert STL rewards to USD using the 24‑hour average price.
3. **Compounding Frequency** – Auto‑compound daily yields a **~2 % boost** over simple APR.

### 6.1 Formula

```
Net APY = ((RewardTokenUSD * (1 - TaxRate) - GasPerCycleUSD) / NFT_ValueUSD) * (365 / CycleDays) * (1 + CompoundingFactor)
```

*Assumptions for example:*

- **NFT value**: $1,200
- **Daily STL reward**: $0.50 (≈ $182.50/year)
- **TaxRate**: 0 % (no capital gains tax for now)
- **GasPerCycleUSD**: $0.05 (once per day)
- **CycleDays**: 1
- **CompoundingFactor**: 0.02 (2 % boost)

```
Net APY = ((0.50 - 0.05) / 1200) * 365 * 1.02
        = (0.45 / 1200) * 372.3
        = 0.000375 * 372.3
        ≈ 13.9 %
```

Even though the advertised APR is **35 %**, the **net APY** after gas and daily compounding lands at **~14 %**, still a solid return for a passive asset.

## 7. Fractional Staking: Unlocking High‑Value NFTs

### 7.1 What Is Fractional Staking?

Instead of staking the whole NFT, you **lock ERC‑20 “shares”** that represent fractional ownership. Platforms like **ApeShares**, **FractionX**, and **MetaFraction** mint these shares after a **vault** locks the original NFT.

| Benefit | Example |
| --- | --- |
| **Lower entry barrier** – Stake $200 worth of shares instead of a $10k NFT | A collector buys 5 % of a Bored Ape for $500, stakes those shares on Starlight |
| **Liquidity** – Shares can be swapped on AMMs while still earning yield | Sell 2 % of shares on QuickSwap, keep the rest staked |
| **Diversification** – Pool multiple high‑value NFTs into a single vault | MetaFraction’s “Blue‑Chip Bundle” contains a Bored Ape, a CryptoPunk, and a Mutant Ape |

### 7.2 How to Stake Fractional Shares

1. **Acquire shares** via the platform’s marketplace or a DEX.
2. **Approve the staking contract** (ERC‑20 `approve`).
3. **Stake the share token** just like any ERC‑20 token (e.g., `STL‑Share`).
4. **Collect rewards** in the native token (STL) plus a **pro‑rata slice of royalties**.

**Important:** Fractional staking does not change the underlying royalty split; you receive a proportional slice of whatever the original NFT would have earned.

## 12. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: Do I need to pay taxes on staked NFT yield?**
A: In most jurisdictions, **token rewards** are treated as ordinary income at the time of receipt, while **royalty payouts** may be considered capital gains when sold. Keep a CSV export from your wallet and consult a tax professional.

**Q2: Can I unstake at any time?**
A: Most L2 protocols allow **instant unstaking** with a minimal gas fee. Some vaults impose a **7‑day cooldown** to protect against flash‑loan attacks.

**Q3: What happens if the NFT’s floor price crashes?**
A: The **token‑only portion** of the reward (e.g., STL emissions) continues unchanged, but **royalty income** may decline. Consider swapping to a lower‑risk pool or adding a stablecoin LP boost.

**Q4: Is staking safe for a $10,000 Bored Ape?**
A: High‑value NFTs are often **wrapped** into a vault (e.g., `ApeVault`) before staking. Ensure the vault has **multi‑sig governance** and **insurance coverage** (e.g., Nexus Mutual).

**Q5: How do I claim rewards on a mobile device?**
A: Most protocols have **responsive web UIs**; you can also use the **MetaMask Mobile** browser. For hardware‑wallet users, connect via **WalletConnect**.
