## What is Quantum AI?
Quantum AI refers to the integration of quantum computing and artificial intelligence. **Quantum computing** is a type of computing that uses quantum-mechanical phenomena, such as superposition and entanglement, to perform operations on data. **Artificial intelligence**, on the other hand, is a field of computer science focused on creating intelligent machines that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. By combining these two technologies, Quantum AI enables faster and more efficient processing of complex data, allowing for breakthroughs in fields such as machine learning, optimization, and simulation.

### Core Concepts
To understand Quantum AI, it's essential to grasp the core concepts of quantum computing and artificial intelligence. Quantum computing uses **qubits** (quantum bits) to process information, which can exist in multiple states simultaneously, allowing for exponential scaling of computing power. Artificial intelligence, on the other hand, relies on **algorithms** and **data** to make decisions and perform tasks. Quantum AI combines these concepts to create a new paradigm for computing and problem-solving.
