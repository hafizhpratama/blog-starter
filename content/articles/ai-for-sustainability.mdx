## Core Concepts and Definitions
To understand the potential of AI for sustainability, it's essential to define what we mean by **sustainability**. According to the United Nations, sustainability refers to **meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs**. In the context of AI, sustainability encompasses a broad range of applications, including **climate change mitigation**, **resource conservation**, and **environmental protection**. AI for sustainability involves the use of artificial intelligence technologies, such as **machine learning** and **deep learning**, to support sustainable development and reduce environmental impact.
