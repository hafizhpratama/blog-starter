## What Everyone Gets Wrong About DeFi Insurance: Dispelling the Myths

* **Claim Assessment Isn't Always Automatic:** While parametric insurance is gaining traction, many protocols still rely on some form of discretionary claim assessment. * **Coverage is Limited:**  Most DeFi insurance protocols don't cover *all* possible risks.  For example, they may not cover losses due to front-running or MEV (Miner Extractable Value).  It's crucial to carefully read the terms and conditions of any insurance policy before purchasing coverage.
* **Undercollateralization is a Concern:** Many insurance protocols operate on a principle of mutualization, where premiums are pooled to cover claims.  If a large-scale exploit occurs, the pool may not be sufficient to cover all losses, potentially leading to partial payouts or even insolvency. * **Oracle Manipulation Still Possible:**  While protocols are working to improve oracle security, manipulation is still a risk.  Attackers may be able to exploit vulnerabilities in oracles to trigger false payouts or deny legitimate claims.
